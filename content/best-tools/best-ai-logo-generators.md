## Overview

AI logo generators have matured significantly, producing professional-quality logos in minutes that once required hiring a designer. In 2026, the best tools go beyond simple icon + text combinations — they offer full brand identity kits, intelligent customization, and commercial-ready files. Whether you're launching a startup or refreshing a side project, here are the top AI logo generators.

## Top 9 AI Logo Generators

### 1. Looka

Looka combines AI logo generation with a complete brand kit platform, generating logos based on your preferences and extending them into business cards, social media kits, and more.

**Key Features:**
- AI logo generation based on industry, style, and color preferences
- 300+ customization options per logo concept
- Full Brand Kit with business cards, social profiles, letterheads
- High-res files (SVG, PNG, EPS, PDF)
- Unlimited revisions before purchase

**Pricing:** Basic Logo $20 (one-time), Premium Logo $65 (one-time), Brand Kit $96/yr

**Pros:** Excellent brand kit ecosystem, high-quality output, generous customization
**Cons:** Best value locked in yearly subscription, some designs feel templated

**Best For:** Startups and small businesses wanting a full brand identity, not just a logo.

## FAQ

**Can AI-generated logos be trademarked?**
Yes, AI-generated logos can generally be trademarked as long as they are sufficiently unique. However, trademark law varies by jurisdiction — consult a trademark attorney for your specific case.

**Are AI logos good enough for professional use?**
Top-tier tools like Looka, Brandmark, and LogoAI produce logos suitable for professional use. For Fortune 500 rebrand quality, you'll still want a human designer.

**Do I own the rights to my AI-generated logo?**
Yes — all listed platforms grant full commercial usage rights upon purchase. Check each platform's license terms for specifics on exclusivity.

**How much should I spend on an AI logo?**
For a startup or small business, $25-65 for a one-time logo purchase is the sweet spot. Brand kit subscriptions ($50-100/yr) add value if you need consistent branded assets.

**Can I edit my AI logo after purchase?**
Most platforms allow editing after purchase. Looka, Turbologo, and LogoAI offer the most flexible post-purchase editing options.

## Conclusion

Looka leads for complete brand identity, Brandmark for creative uniqueness, and Hatchful for zero-budget needs.
