# GPT-5.3 Released: 10 AI Tools That Will Be Affected

## GPT-5.3 Update Highlights

On March 3, 2026, OpenAI released the GPT-5.3 Instant update, following the February 5 launch of GPT-5.3-Codex, a coding-specialized model. GPT-5.3 is the third iteration of the GPT-5 series, bringing significant performance improvements.

**Core Updates:**
- **400K context window**: 4x larger, supports entire novels, complete legal documents, large codebases
- **25% speed boost**: GPT-5.3-Codex is 25% faster
- **Stronger reasoning**: Reduced factual errors, improved problem-solving
- **More natural conversation**: Fixed "preachy tone," "defensive preambles"
- **Enhanced multimodal**: Better image/video/audio understanding
- **Long-term memory**: Remembers past conversations, preferences, workflows

**Release Timeline:**
- Late January 2026: Beta for ChatGPT Pro subscribers
- February 2026: Full API availability
- March 2026: Free tier integration
- March 3, 2026: GPT-5.3 Instant update released
