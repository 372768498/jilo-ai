## Why Look for Fireflies.ai Alternatives?

Fireflies.ai is a popular AI meeting assistant that records, transcribes, and summarizes meetings. Users explore alternatives for various reasons:

- **Accuracy concerns** — Transcription quality varies across accents and technical jargon
- **Privacy considerations** — Some teams are uncomfortable with a bot joining calls
- **Storage limits** — Free and lower-tier plans restrict recording storage
- **Platform support** — Need integration with specific video conferencing tools
- **Feature needs** — Want better action item tracking or CRM integration
- **Pricing per seat** — Costs scale quickly for larger teams
- **Data residency** — Enterprise requirements for data storage location

## Top 10 Fireflies.ai Alternatives

### 1. Otter.ai

**Best for:** Real-time transcription with collaborative note-taking

**Description:** Otter.ai is one of the most recognized AI transcription tools, offering real-time transcription during meetings with collaborative editing features. Its OtterPilot automatically joins and transcribes meetings.

**Key Features:**
- Real-time transcription during meetings
- OtterPilot auto-joins Zoom, Teams, and Google Meet
- Collaborative editing and commenting on transcripts
- AI-generated meeting summaries and action items
- Otter AI Chat to ask questions about your meetings

**Pricing:** Free plan with 300 min/month; Pro at $16.99/month; Business at $30/month; Enterprise custom

**Best for:** Teams wanting real-time collaborative transcription

### 2. Tactiq

**Best for:** Live transcription with instant AI summaries

**Description:** Tactiq provides live captions and transcription directly in your browser, without requiring a bot to join your meeting. It works as a Chrome extension, making it lightweight and privacy-friendly.

**Key Features:**
- Browser-based transcription (no bot joins the meeting)
- Works with Google Meet, Zoom, and MS Teams
- AI meeting summaries with custom prompts
- Speaker identification and labeling
- Export to Google Docs, Notion, and Slack

**Pricing:** Free plan with 5 transcripts/month; Pro at $12/month; Team at $20/user/month

**Best for:** Privacy-conscious teams who don't want bots in meetings

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best free alternative to Fireflies.ai?
Fathom offers completely free unlimited recording and transcription for individual users, making it the best free alternative. tl;dv also provides a generous free plan with unlimited recordings.

### Which alternative doesn't require a bot to join meetings?
Tactiq and Krisp both work without sending a bot to your meetings. Tactiq uses a Chrome extension for browser-based transcription, while Krisp works at the system audio level.

### What's the most accurate meeting transcription tool?
Otter.ai and Fathom consistently rank highest for transcription accuracy in English. For multilingual meetings, tl;dv offers strong accuracy across 30+ languages.

### Which alternative is best for sales teams?
Avoma and Chorus are purpose-built for sales conversation intelligence. Grain and Fathom also offer strong CRM integrations for logging meeting outcomes to Salesforce or HubSpot.

### Can these tools transcribe in-person meetings?
Most tools focus on virtual meetings, but Otter.ai has a mobile app that can record and transcribe in-person conversations. Krisp can also work with any audio source on your device.

## Conclusion

Meeting transcription tools have become essential for modern teams, and there's no shortage of Fireflies.ai alternatives. **Fathom** is unbeatable for individuals with its free unlimited plan. **Otter.ai** leads in real-time collaborative transcription. **Tactiq** is the privacy-first choice with no bot required.

For sales teams, **Avoma** and **Grain** offer the best conversation intelligence and CRM integration. **tl;dv** excels for international teams with strong multilingual support. Most tools offer free plans or trials — start there and upgrade once you've found your fit.
