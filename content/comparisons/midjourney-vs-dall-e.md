## FAQ

### Which tool produces higher quality images?

Midjourney generally produces higher quality artistic images with better composition and aesthetic appeal. DALL-E excels at photorealistic images and technical accuracy, especially for text rendering.

### Is Midjourney harder to use than DALL-E?

Yes, significantly. Midjourney requires Discord, uses command-line style prompts, and has a steep learning curve. DALL-E integrates with ChatGPT and uses natural language, making it much more accessible.

### Which is better for commercial use?

DALL-E is better for most commercial applications due to its user-friendly interface, commercial licensing included in all plans, and reliable performance. Midjourney requires Pro/Mega plans for commercial use.

### Can I get professional-quality results from both tools?

Yes, both can produce professional-quality results, but for different purposes. Midjourney excels at artistic and creative professional work, while DALL-E is better for business and marketing applications.

### Which tool is more cost-effective?

DALL-E is more cost-effective for occasional use through ChatGPT Plus ($20/month) or API usage. Midjourney is better value for heavy users who benefit from unlimited relaxed generations.
