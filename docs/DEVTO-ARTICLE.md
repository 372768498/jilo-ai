## The Surprising Findings

1. **Free tiers are more generous than you think.** Claude, Gemini, and Leonardo AI all have solid free options.

2. **The "best" tool depends entirely on your use case.** ChatGPT wins for general tasks, Claude wins for writing, Cursor wins for coding.

3. **Most people only need 2-3 AI tools.** Don't subscribe to everything.

4. **Prices change constantly.** This is why I built a directory that stays updated.
